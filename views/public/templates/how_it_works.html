I am a freelancer ! How should I start ?

  1. Give Qualification Test

    2. At the time of signup with ClientDev, you have to pass a Q-Test which would assess your certain abilities such as programming and general computer knowledge. Only after clearing the Q-Test you will be able to create your account.

  2. Manage your Profile and browse projects

    3. After successfull signup with ClientDev, manage your profile by adding your skills and other information. Browse projects of your interest and try to pass E-Test set up by the Clients. Clear E-Test and increase your chances of getting hired.

  3. Perform Well, Get your rewards !

    4. If you get hired, execute your tasks well and impress Cleint with your work. Get rewards if any. Get your ClientDev Certi for sure.
